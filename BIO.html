Welcome to Dr. Chavez's Biography page

Dr. Chavez was born and raised in Caracas, Venezuela. She completed Medical School at Universidad Central de Venezuela. After completing a year working as a general physician in a rural area, she moved to USA to persue training in Surgery. Then, she completed an HepatoPancreatoBiliary Fellowship and currently works in Nashville, TN.

She is married to Giovanni Davogustto, whom he met during Med School. He's a Cardiology Fellow at Vanderbilt University. They have an 8 months old son named Nino.
